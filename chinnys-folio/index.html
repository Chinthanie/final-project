My Story

While completing a psychology degree, I found myself yearning for a creative outlet. In considering the connection between creativity and the human mind, I found communication design. By building my design knowledge and skills over the past four years, I have come to appreciate the intimate relationship between psychology and design. These range from the relevance of Gestalt principles and the aesthetic qualities of effective design to the relevance of cognitive and behavioural science in marketing and design strategy. I am drawn to advertising, branding, packaging and UI/UX where understanding audience psychology to motivate and persuade is vital.
